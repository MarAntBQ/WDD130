Meet our Services

Welcome to ChilaChito! We are your ultimate destination for an unforgettable rafting experience. Our professional team is dedicated to providing you with safe and exhilarating adventures on the river. With our state-of-the-art equipment and expert guides, you can immerse yourself in the thrill of navigating through thrilling rapids while enjoying the stunning landscapes. Join us at ChilaChito and get ready to make lifelong memories on the water.

Experience the thrill of our rapid river adventures as you navigate through challenging rapids and embrace the power of nature.

Wilderness Camp

Immerse yourself in nature at our serene campsite, where you can unwind, connect with the outdoors, and create lasting memories.

Rapid Thrills

Embark on an exhilarating journey of white-water rafting, conquering thrilling rapids and embracing the adrenaline-fueled excitement.
